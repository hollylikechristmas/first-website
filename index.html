A little bit about me.

I am a Pacific Northwest native and lover of the outdoors, raised and currently living in Portland, OR with my amazing husband and infant son.
I made a foray into coding and devlopment after feeling unfulfilled in my former career as a corporate designer, delving into online classes to learn HTML, CSS, and responsive web/app design and realizing what a great fit this new path had become.

That first jump into coding then opened a Pandora's Box of learning, passion, and endless creative problem-solving. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the opportunity to be able to design and build any website from scratch. Now I've made a full switch to front-end developement, where I can use my design background and coding skills to create beautiful custom websites.
